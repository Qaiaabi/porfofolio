About Me

Hello! My name is Qaia Abiyasha, a passionate student currently studying at SMK Negeri 1 Bantul, where I major in Software Engineering (RPL).

Ever since I embarked on this journey, I've been deeply fascinated by the digital world — especially in areas like web design, UI/UX design, and web development. I’ve not only explored these fields but also honed my skills to a professional level, building real-world projects with modern technologies.

Among the websites I've developed, one notable project is Dusun Tulung, an official information hub for Dusun Tulung, located in Srihardono Village, Pundong District, Bantul, Yogyakarta. This platform serves as a digital window into the community, providing local news, cultural insights, and updates.

Another significant project of mine is Arthasiddha - Eclathaus, a curated product catalog and art gallery website. Based in Kajen, Bantul, Yogyakarta, it showcases the richness of Indonesian craftsmanship, including pottery, wooden furniture, and terrazzo creations. With a sleek and modern UI, the platform bridges traditional artistry with modern design, functioning as both a catalog and a business contact point.

I also have experience building a Library Management System using the Laravel 11 framework — a project you can explore on my Github. This project helped me dive deeper into backend systems and real-world database interaction.

In every project I take on, I aim to create experiences that are not only functional but also visually captivating. I believe that great design is where art meets logic — and that's exactly where I thrive.
